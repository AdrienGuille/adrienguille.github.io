Research

I'm part of the Data Mining & Decision Group at the ERIC Lab. My research interests lie in the development of machine learning and data mining techniques for the analysis and modeling of text, with the aim of solving real-world problems related to language.
Check out my work on document network embedding and social media mining or my work in sociolinguistics.
